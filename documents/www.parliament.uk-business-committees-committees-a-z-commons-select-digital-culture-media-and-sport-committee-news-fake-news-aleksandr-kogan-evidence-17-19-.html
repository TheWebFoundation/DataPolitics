Dr Aleksandr Kogan questioned by Committee

24 April 2018

The Digital, Culture, Media and Sport Committee questions Dr Aleksandr Kogan from the Department of Psychology at the University of Cambridge, as part of its inquiry into fake news.

  • Watch Parliament TV: Fake news
  • Inquiry: Fake news
  • Digital, Culture, Media and Sport Committee

Witness

Tuesday 24 April 2018, Grimond Room, Portcullis House

At 10.30am

  • Dr Aleksandr Kogan, Senior Research Associate, Department of Psychology, Cambridge University

Focus of the session

MPs use the opportunity to question Dr Kogan about his company Global Science Research (GSR) and its relationship with Cambridge Analytica amongst other issues.
